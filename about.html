Who We Are

HopeForAll is a global nonprofit organization dedicated to creating lasting change in the lives of children, families, and communities living in poverty and injustice. Founded in 2005, we've grown from a small grassroots initiative to an international organization with operations in over 30 countries.

Our approach is holistic, addressing the root causes of poverty through sustainable solutions in education, healthcare, economic development, and emergency relief. We believe in empowering communities to become self-sufficient rather than creating dependency.

Global Reach

Operating in 36 countries across 5 continents

Community Focus

Working with local leaders to create sustainable change

Transparency

90% of donations go directly to programs

Recognition

Multiple awards for effectiveness and transparency

Our Mission & Vision

We believe in a world where every person has the opportunity to thrive, not just survive. Our mission is to empower communities to break the cycle of poverty through sustainable solutions.

Our Mission

To create sustainable change in the lives of vulnerable children, families, and communities by addressing the root causes of poverty and injustice through holistic, community-led solutions.

Our Vision

A world where every person has access to basic needs, quality education, healthcare, and economic opportunities to reach their full potential and contribute to their communities.

Our Values

Compassion, Integrity, Accountability, Empowerment, and Collaboration guide every decision we make and every action we take as an organization.
